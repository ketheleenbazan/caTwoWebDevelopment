About us!

Mission Africa was founded in 2012 after a trip that one of our founders made to Mozambique at the invitation of a local institution. As soon as she returned to Ireland, the idea came up with some friends, then founded the ONG Mission Africa, with the aim of transforming the reality of people who live in poverty and vulnerability.

Mission Africa has its headquarters in Dublin city and we currently have work being carried out in Ireland and Mozambique.

The entire body of directors of the ONG performs its functions on a voluntary basis.
