Our Mission


Contribute to the growth of people in Africa fighting poverty and hunger.

Click on the images below to read more.


At the moment we have 45 volunteers/sponsors around the world that helps transform the reality of children in this community. From 15 euros a month you can contribute to a future of hope, providing food, medicine and improvements where they live.

Most of the vegetables offered at our nutritional center are produced in our community gardens. In addition, we have already drilled several wells in the communities served, which has improved the quality of life for thousands of people.

Our nutritional center is a reference in the work of recovery and nutritional monitoring of children with acute malnutrition. We have worked tirelessly to provide the best in terms of food and professional support.

Volunteer action: in order to be efficient and effective, it must be based on the principle of solidarity and must enhance social inclusion and the reduction of inequalities. We often have volunteers at the community contribute to make their community a better place to live.

The lack of clothes is among the reasons that prevent children from the poor community from going to school and thus increases the school drop-out rate.

Donations for winter time have also been helping our community a lot, especially during the night time that is mostly cold. Any help makes a big difference.
